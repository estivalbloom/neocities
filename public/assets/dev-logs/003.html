So this past week or so the first big thing I've been working on for the website is adding minesweeper. It just doesn't feel like I'm really mimicking windows 98 properly if you can't play minesweeper. Eventually I plan to at least add solitaire as well, but I'm not really in any rush for that one. Something I've been making a concious effort about with this site is that I try to get the interface and UI to be a pixel-perfect match to windows 98, but all of the sprites and other assets I make from scratch. I had a lot of fun with the minesweeper sprites; since the scoreboard on minesweeper mimics a 7-segment display, I went with a weird 8-segment display I learned about from a Technology Connections video. It kind of sucks in my opinion, most of the numbers get a slightly prettier shape but the 0 and the 6 in particular I think look horrible. However, for me, it sucks in just the right retro-tech way. It's grown on me and I love it now, and if I didn't want things to be kinda shitty I wouldn't be designing my entire website to mimic Windows 98 :). I'm also pretty proud of the execution on my 8-segment font; I spent a good while experimenting with it to get it reading the right way.
